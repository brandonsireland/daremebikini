Ordering & Return Policy

All Sales Are Final. No Refunds.

We will exchange the size or fabric only, and it must be within 10 days of your delivery date. The items must still have all the tags in place and must not have been worn, or laundered. In order to do an exchange you'll need to first call us to be assigned an Authorization Number. This Number must be clearly written on the front of the package you return to us, otherwise we cannot accept it.


Note - we must receive the item you are exchanging within 10 days of the time we issued you the return authorization number, after the 10 days the return authorization will be void.

Once we receive your return we will first determine that it has been sent back to us in the same perfect condition in which it was shipped to you. Any customer doing excessive exchanges will be taken off of our client list and we would no longer be able purchase from Dare Me Bikini. Return shipping charges will be billed to your credit card.

Cancellation Policy:
Once an order is placed you may not cancel an order. In rare circumstances you may be able to stop shipping by contacting: Dare Me Bikini's Customer Service M-F, 11 to 7pm PST at 714-842-9444.
THIS DOES NOT GUARANTEE that the order will be cancelled. Dare Me Bikini does not accept any form of cancellations by email.
